Instructions

A wizard (he seemed kinda odd...) handed me this . Can you figure out what it says?

Hints

There are tools that make this easy this.

Solution

This one was a throwback. Me and my online friends would send messages like this to eachother and we would see who could decode it the fastest. Eventually, I found something called "quipquip.com". This is what I used to decipher this challenge. I copied the text they gave me, and started comparing each 3 letter groupings against the word "THE". It did not take long, considering I said that the first grouping of three "MIT" equaled "THE". The only difficult part was remembering the name of the website.
